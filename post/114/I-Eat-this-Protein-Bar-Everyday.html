Title

I Eat this Protein Bar Everyday

Published:11/15/2017
Article

The U.S. Dietary Guidlines state that an “adult man should take a minimum of 10% of his daily calories from protien”, and if you’re someone like me who needs a quick way to get some protein into your diet, then I’d highly recommend Clif Builder’s Protein bars.

I’ve tried a number of protein bars:Probars,thinkThin, toOh Yeah!, and I’ve settled on Clif Builder’s Protein bars based on taste, more organic/natural ingredients, and price.

Taste

Unlike a lot of protein bars, Clif Builder’s Protein bars do not use artificial sweeteners in their product. Obviously, this will increase the calories (almost all of their bars are 270-280 calories/bar) and carbohydrates. But when you eat their bar, it doesn’t taste like you are eating aproteinbar. There’s no protein aftertaste; it literally tastes like your eating a candy bar with 20g of soy based protein.

I really like thechocolateflavor, but I tend to eat the chocolate mint and chocolate peanut butter most often causeSam’s Clubcarries those two flavors in bulk (~$20/box of 18). As of writing this article, they sell five flavors (chocolate mint, chocolate peanut butter, cookies n’ cream, crunchy peanut butter, and vanilla almond) of this protein bar and they just released awhey protein bar version.

Speaking of protein, Clif uses soy protein isolate which is a godsend since my stomach and whey don’t really get along (probably due to mychinese heritage). They have a decent number of organic ingredients and it packs a number of vitamins & minerals (You can find more info on Clif’s websitehere). I know tha GMO foods aregenerally safe for you, but I personally like eating things where I can understand the ingredient list rather than looking up every other word. And just like how whey protein can wreak havoc on my gastrointenstinal tract, some artificial sweeteners have a similar affect on me; so these bars are a win-win for me.

Price $$$

I am definitely a price sensitive consumer with very little brand loyalty (bite me), so I was thrilled to find out that I could purchase Clif Builder’s Bars from my localSam’s Club storeat around $1.11 per bar, even Amazon at one point were selling these bars at $13.58 per box of 12 this past year.


Price graph taken fromcamelcamelcamel.com

I love these bars and I eat theseeveryday, it’s probably fair to say that I’m 10% Clif Builder Protein Bar since you are what you eat :). If you get a chance, I’d recommend you try them. It’s like eating a candy bar with 20g protein. Bliss.
