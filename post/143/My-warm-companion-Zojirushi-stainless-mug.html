Title

My warm companion: Zojirushi stainless mug

Published:11/17/2017
Article

BRRRRR!! (shaking) It’s that time of the year where I’m wishing for some warmer weather. Thankfully I have my16oz Zojirushi Stainless Steel
Mugto transfer some warm liquids(mainly water) into my body. There are somepseudo-benefitsto drinking warm water as well but personally I drink warm water cause it warms up my body (I know right).

Keeps Water Warm

If there’s something that the Zojirushi mug does well, it is keeping water warm. The mug keeps the water so warm that some people at times will complain about how
toowarm the water stays (which is a dumb reason to complain tbh). I suspect that it accomplishes this through the stellar design of the lid. I can fill up hot liquids at 8am and it will literally stay hot until 8pm. And when you pair this mug with a
hot water boiler/warmer, it becomes a winning combo against cold weather.

Design Design Design

As the name suggests, the entire body of the mug is stainless steel, however the lid is made of plastic/rubber material. You can unscrew the lid entirely off the bottle, and the lid itself can be taken apart into four simple parts which make it
super supereasy to clean (as opposed to thosestrawed contigo
water bottlesthat are diffult to wash). The lid also has a locking mechanism so that it will
neverspill in your bag.


The mug looks sharp. It definitely has a premium look and feel to it; and I guess it should since it retails foraround
$27. I’ve had my Zojirushi for over a year now and I can see minimal scratches/indents/blemishes; it’s holding up quite well.

Tip #1 – snap back lid
The lid itself can snap backwards by pushing the lid all the way back which holds the lid in the open position until you snap the lid back forward. I didn’t realize this until I accidentally happened; it was a nice surprise, and it’s super helpful since the lid won’t accidentally hit you in the face when you are pouring hot scorching liquids down your throat

Tip #2 – protect your investment
Be sure to tag/write your name on the mug somewhere just in case you lose it. I put my name and my phone # on it using a piece of paper and some scotch tape. Took 10 minutes, which could save you from buying another one. Also side benefit of letting other people know your phone #, if that’s what you’re into :P.
